Correlation of Suicide rates against Economical, Social, and Spiritual Factors

In this presentation I try to find which factors correlates the most with suicide rates across the world. Data for suicide rates comes from Wikipedia: http://goo.gl/MVLE

The App can be found at: http://omargomez.shinyapps.io/Quandl1/

Suicide vs Economical Factors

The least correlation of this study

  • List of countries by GDP (PPP) per capita
  • List of countries by unemployment rate

Suicide vs Social factors

Not too bad

  • Divorce demography
  • Age at First Marriage - Female By Country
  • List of countries by number of households

Suicide vs Spiritual factors

The most correlation

  • Irreligion by country
  • Importance of religion by country
